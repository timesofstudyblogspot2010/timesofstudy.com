River Brahmaputra


Recently, the state government of Assam has inaugurated a 1.8 km ropeway across the river Brahmaputra and described it as the country's longest river ropeway.
The Brahmaputra, called Yarlung Tsangpo in Tibet, Siang/Dihang River in Arunachal Pradesh and Luit, Dilao in Assam, is a trans-boundary river which flows through China, India and Bangladesh. It is the 4th largest river in the world by discharge, and the 15th longest.
